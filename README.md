# Orbit Girls College

A private girls college in Pakistan offering Intermediate and Bachelors programs in a safe, disciplined environment.

## Overview
Orbit Girls College provides quality education for female students from FSc/ICS/I.Com to BS level programs. Multiple campuses in Karachi, Lahore, Islamabad.

## Programs Offered

### Intermediate - FSc/FA/ICS/I.Com
- **FSc Pre-Medical**: Biology, Chemistry, Physics
- **FSc Pre-Engineering**: Math, Physics, Chemistry  
- **ICS**: Computer Science, Statistics, Math/Physics
- **I.Com**: Accounting, Economics, Business Math
- **FA General**: Arts subjects

### Bachelors Programs
- **BS Computer Science** - 4 Years
- **BS Information Technology** - 4 Years
- **BBA** - Business Administration
- **BS English, Psychology** 
- **ADP** - Associate Degree Programs

## Key Features
- **Girls Only Campus**: Safe learning environment
- **Affiliation**: BIEK Karachi / Local Universities
- **Discipline**: Strict attendance, uniform policy
- **Facilities**: Computer labs, Science labs, Library
- **Faculty**: Qualified female teachers

## Admission Requirements

| Program | Min Marks | Documents |
| --- | --- | --- |
| FSc Pre-Eng/Med | 60%+ in Matric | Matric Result, B-Form, Photos |
| ICS/I.Com/FA | 50%+ in Matric | CNIC Copy of Guardian |
| BS Programs | 50%+ in Inter | Inter Result, CNIC Copies |

## Fee Structure
**Intermediate**: Rs 8,000 - 15,000/month  
**Bachelors**: Rs 15,000 - 25,000/month  
*Varies by campus & program. Scholarships available for position holders.*
